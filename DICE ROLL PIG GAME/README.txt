Pig Dice Game (Python)  
======================

Description:
------------
This is a simple multiplayer "Pig Dice Game" implemented in Python.  
Players take turns rolling a 6-sided die to earn points. The first player to reach or exceed 50 points wins.

Rules:
------
1. 2–4 players can play.
2. On a player's turn:
   - They can roll the die as many times as they like.
   - If they roll a number between 2–6, it is added to their current round score.
   - If they roll a 1, their current round score becomes 0, and their turn ends immediately.
   - A player can choose to stop rolling at any time and add their round score to their total score.
3. The first player to reach 50 or more points wins.
